A solid skin repair bar for hands that take a beating. FlitzGrip is made from seven organic ingredients. No water, no fragrance, no fillers. The wax base melts on contact, sealing cracks and fissures while restoring the natural oils stripped by chalk, rope, and training.

The clip-on teardrop container attaches directly to your chalk bag. 18g of bar lasts 3–4 months of regular use.
Cera Alba, Simmondsia Chinensis Seed Oil, Butyrospermum Parkii Butter, Persea Gratissima Oil, Cocos Nucifera Oil, Tocopherol, Hypericum Perforatum Flower Extract.

All ingredients are certified organic. No synthetic additives, preservatives, or artificial fragrances.
  • Wash and dry hands thoroughly after training
  • Rub the bar directly onto damaged skin. Focus on fingertips, fissures, and callus edges
  • A small amount goes a long way. No need to rinse
  • Apply before sleep for best results; the wax works while you rest
  • Clip the container to your chalk bag for easy access at the wall
