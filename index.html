How To Use

Three steps.
Works while you recover.

Step 01

After training

Wash your hands and let them dry completely. For best results, apply before sleep. Your hands repair themselves overnight.

Step 02

Apply directly

Rub the bar directly onto dry, damaged skin. A small amount goes a long way. Focus on fissures, tips, and callus edges.

Step 03

Recover overnight

No need to rinse. The wax seals and works as you rest. Clip back to your chalk bag, ready for the next session.

Our Story

We built this because we needed it.

We're a climbing family based in Berlin. After the initial honeymoon phase with the sport, our hands started taking real damage. We tried plenty of good products out there, but we were looking for a very specific feel. We wanted something solid, completely waterless, and stripped down to the essentials. So we made our own.

What started in our kitchen was just meant for our own gym bags. But after sharing it with friends and family and seeing their reaction, we realized other people were looking for that exact same feel.

FlitzGrip is our take on skin repair. Seven organic ingredients, poured by hand into a solid bar. No water, no extra noise, just a simple, considered tool. Made with care in Berlin.
